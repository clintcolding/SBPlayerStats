Frequently Asked Questions


Are teams and players automatically added to the leaderboards?

Teams and players in PL1 or PL2 are automatically added at the beginning of each season.

How can I add my team to the leaderboards?

You can add your team by searching or directly visiting your team page.

When are stats updated?

Stats are automatically updated for each team daily at 11 PM EST.

Can I update my teams stats?

Yes, individual team stats are updated each time their page is visited.

Are stats persistent?

Not yet. Currently, SmallStats only displays data from the current SmallBall season.

Where can I ask a question or give feedback?

There is an active thread on the SmallBall forums where you can get help or leave feedback.
